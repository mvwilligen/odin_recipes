Lasagna


ingredients

  • minced meat
  • chopped vegetables
  • grated cheese
  • lasagna pasta leaves
  • tomato puree
  • italian spices
  • butter

steps

  1. pre-heat oven: 200 degrees celsius
  2. prepare the meat
  3. add spices
  4. add vegetables
  5. butter the tray
  6. layer up, sauce, leaves, sauce, leaves
  7. add grated cheese
  8. in oven for 30 mins
